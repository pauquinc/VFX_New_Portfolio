    Projects

    Fresh Vibes Fest Aftermovie

    The afterevent video of Fresh Vibes Fiesta captured the event concept, which was a kaleidoscope of energy and celebration. This creation blended dynamic motion graphics, fluid transitions, and bold typography. Collaboration and iterative refinement ensured a visually stunning experience, perfectly reflecting the festival's spirit.

    "How To Listen to This Album" Music Video

    The animation for "How to Listen to This Album" by Stereoclip embraced the song's introspective journey. I fused mesmerizing visuals with emotive storytelling, guiding viewers through surreal and cosmics landscapes. resulting in a visually captivating experience that echoed the song's melodic depth and emotional resonance.

    Haven Fest Posters

    "Haven Fest's" posters seamlessly merges design and music, with lines in motion symbolizing the dynamic flow of sound and creativity. I crafted dynamic visuals intertwining with the rhythm of the beats, creating an immersive experience. Collaboration ensured a captivating fusion, echoing the festival's spirited convergence of artistry and melody.

    Costafe Brand book

    Crafting this brand book was a creative journey where every element had to reflect the essence of Costafe. I started by immersing ourselves in the company's spirit, carefully selecting colors and designing a layout inspired by the elegance of a coffee cup. The result? A visual manifesto that embodies Costafe's passion for great coffee.

    Pixie's Article Layout

    Designing the Pixies' article layout was a vibrant journey. Bright colors infused each page with energy, reflecting the band's dynamic presence. Inspired by their iconic style, I crafted a layout that celebrated their music, blending bold imagery with sleek typography.

    Ukelele's guide card holder

    I designed this ukulele card case to be as useful as it is vibrant. It started with a desire to create a solution for the loose and disorganized guide cards. I explored bold color palettes, aiming to transform the case into a visual reference system. Each hue acts as a cue, helping players quickly identify the chord they need. The result? A functional design that injects a touch of joy into every practice session.

    Marketing Campaign Costafe

    This project involved crafting a multi-platform marketing campaign for a coffee brand. I aimed to reflect a cohesive brand identity across email, apparel, and social media. The email campaign, for instance, featured imagery that mirrored the promo item designs, while social media posts used playful graphics to engage followers and promote the brand's story.

    "La Madremonte" Children's book

    This project involved creating the layout for a children's book focused on environmental responsibility. My goal was to visually support the story's message of caring for nature. Green, in all its various shades, became the hero of the colour palette. This colour scheme served as a constant reminder of the beauty and importance of the natural world. The layout itself was designed to be engaging to young readers, subtly reinforcing the story's message through the use of colour.

    Opalo Houses Booklet

    This project involved crafting a booklet designed to showcase some properties for sale. I prioritized a balance between elegance and information density. Clean, modern layouts with ample white space allowed the renders of each property to take center stage. A clear colour-coding system differentiated property types, while strategically placed call-to-action elements. The result was a visually compelling and user-friendly booklet that entices readers to explore their dream property.

    Dairy Drop UX experience Design

    Conducting UX research for sustainable milk delivery was an enriching journey. From crafting personas to prototyping, each step aimed to enhance user experience while respecting and integrating brand guidelines. Delving into user needs guided every decision, ensuring a seamless and eco-conscious delivery system. The result is a solution that reflects the companie's commitment to sustainability, user-centered design, and brand integrity.

    Annals of Acicom

    Designing the layout for the congress memory books was a delicate balance between bright colors and a scholarly feel. I opted for clean lines and elegant design, infusing just the right amount of vibrancy to keep it engaging yet dignified. Each page exudes an academic aura, while pops of color add a touch of liveliness, resulting in a series of books that strike the perfect harmony between intellect and visual appeal.

    Two-Way Magazine Layout

    Crafting the layout for the biking magazine was a journey through bohemian and eclectic vibes. I aimed for a blend of laid-back charm and urban chic, infusing the pages with vibrant colors and whimsical typography. Each spread exudes a free-spirited energy, inviting readers on a stylish cycling adventure.
